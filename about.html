About Xingashe Uniforms

Xingashe Trading is a 100% black youth female-owned company based in Engcobo, Eastern Cape. We specialize in the production and supply of uniforms and paraphernalia for schools, churches, and other organizations. We also offer branding services, including embroidery and other printing options.

Our Objectives

  • Produce quality school uniforms for local schools at reasonable prices.
  • Ensure uniforms are readily available and delivered on time.
  • Reduce excessive travel costs for parents and learners in rural communities.
  • Maintain high-quality standards and correct specifications for uniforms.
