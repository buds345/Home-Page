About Xingashe Uniforms

Xingashe Trading is a 100% black youth female-owned company based in Engcobo, Eastern Cape. We specialize in the production and supply of uniforms and paraphernalia for schools, churches, and other organizations. Our services extend beyond uniforms to include branding options such as embroidery and various printing techniques.

Founded with the vision to support local communities, Xingashe Trading is committed to providing high-quality, affordable uniforms that meet the needs of educational institutions, religious organizations, and businesses. Our products are designed with durability, comfort, and style in mind, ensuring that they serve the needs of our customers effectively.

We understand the unique challenges faced by rural communities, especially in accessing quality educational materials and uniforms. Our mission is to bridge this gap by delivering reliable, cost-effective solutions that reduce the burden on parents and students.

Our Objectives

  • Produce quality school uniforms for local schools at reasonable prices.
  • Ensure uniforms are readily available and delivered on time.
  • Reduce excessive travel costs for parents and learners in rural communities.
  • Maintain high-quality standards and correct specifications for uniforms.

Our team is dedicated to continuous improvement and innovation, ensuring that our products not only meet but exceed customer expectations. We believe in fostering strong relationships with our clients, built on trust, quality, and exceptional service.
